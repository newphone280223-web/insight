About Our Startup

India's specialized Duplex Stainless Steel manufacturing venture

We are establishing a manufacturing unit dedicated to processing duplex stainless steel billets and blooms into finished and semi-finished products. India is the second-largest consumer and the third-largest producer of stainless steel globally, with estimated installed capacity between 6.6 to 6.8 million tons.

Our vision aligns with India's target to reach 20 MTPA by 2047, with projected 5.5 million tons by 2030 at 6% CAGR. China currently accounts for 53.3% of global crude steel production, while India's share is approximately 7.9%, presenting significant growth opportunity.

What is Duplex Steel?

50% Austenite and 50% Ferrite microstructure

Corrosion Resistance

Better resistance to SCC (Stress Corrosion Cracking) and Pitting compared to standard stainless steel

Strength & Toughness

Double yield strength and better toughness than standard stainless steel

Composition

Higher chromium (20-28%) and molybdenum with lower nickel content than stainless steel

Manufacturing Process

From raw material to finished product

1. Melting & Refining

EAF/AOD process: Scrap & alloys melted; Nitrogen precisely injected. Ultra-low Carbon (<0.03%) and balanced chemistry.

2. Casting & Hot Working

Continuous casting into slabs/billets followed by hot rolling to create elongated "stringers" of Ferrite and Austenite.

3. Solution Annealing

Rapid heating (1040-1100°C) followed by immediate water quench to freeze 50/50 phase balance and prevent Sigma Phase.

4. Finishing & QC

Pickling (acid bath) and NDT (Ultrasonic/Eddy Current) testing for surface passivity and structural integrity.
